## 🚨 Crisis Communication Leadership

### Crisis Communication Framework

#### Crisis Timeline Management
```
EXECUTIVE CRISIS COMMUNICATION PHASES:

PHASE 1: IMMEDIATE RESPONSE (0-4 Hours)
Immediate Actions Checklist:
☐ Assess situation severity and potential impact
☐ Activate crisis communication team and protocols
☐ Draft initial holding statement with key facts
☐ Establish information gathering and verification processes
☐ Notify legal, compliance, and executive leadership
☐ Prepare for stakeholder notification cascade

Crisis Assessment Framework:
- What happened and when?
- Who is affected and how severely?
- Is the situation contained or still evolving?
- What are potential implications and consequences?
- What resources are needed for response?

PHASE 2: STAKEHOLDER NOTIFICATION (4-24 Hours)
Internal Stakeholder Communication:
- Executive team briefing and role assignment
- Department head notification and guidance
- Employee communication and support provision
- Board member notification (if material impact)
- Investor relations preparation

External Stakeholder Notification:
- Key customer and client personal notification
- Strategic partner and vendor coordination
- Regulatory body notification (if required)
- Media relations preparation and spokesperson designation

PHASE 3: PUBLIC COMMUNICATION (24-72 Hours)
Public Statement Elements:
- Acknowledge situation with appropriate concern level
- Provide factual information without speculation
- Outline immediate actions being taken
- Address stakeholder impact and support measures
- Commit to ongoing updates and transparency

Communication Channels:
- Official website and social media updates
- Media interviews and press conferences
- Customer and client direct communication
- Industry association and partner coordination
- Employee and stakeholder town halls

PHASE 4: ONGOING MANAGEMENT (1-4 Weeks)
Continuous Communication:
- Regular progress updates and milestone achievement
- Proactive addressing of emerging questions and concerns
- Social media monitoring and response management
- Stakeholder feedback collection and incorporation
- Communication strategy adjustment based on outcomes

PHASE 5: RESOLUTION AND RECOVERY (4+ Weeks)
Recovery Communication:
- Announce resolution or significant mitigation
- Communicate lessons learned and improvements implemented
- Demonstrate enhanced capabilities and prevention measures
- Rebuild stakeholder confidence through transparency
- Establish ongoing communication and improvement protocols
```
